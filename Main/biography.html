Biography

Hello! My name is Ethan Cline, and I am a passionate student Game Developer at Boise State University. I specialize in Programming, Level Design, and World Design.

From a young age, I was fascinated by the way games tell stories and create immersive worlds. My journey into game development started with experimenting in Unity and soon spread to Unreal Engine, and since then, I've developed multiple projects that blend creativity and technical skill.

Outside of game development, I enjoy exploring new technologies, designing interactive experiences, and pushing the boundaries of what games can achieve.

My specialties are mainly in coding; I am familiar with C#, JavaScript, Python, CSS, HTML, Java, and Lua.

I also have skills in Blender, as I animate my projects there as well, and have some knowledge of Adobe Photoshop, Animate, Premiere Pro, and Illustrator.
